About Us

During the pandemic, economic activity was down and surely unemployment was increasing. Me, thinking of a way to help those in need of a job, I researched on the sectors I can help. The one i saw most opportunity in was freelance services, right now freelancers can't find enough work and are managing an Irregular Income. So my solution was to create an app which is more transparent and has a faster search process. And that is how QuickLance was created.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To provide a safe place where job opportunities may come. To be a help in the process of people getting jobs.

Mission

QuickLance's mission is to supercharge your hiring process. Turning freelancing to another level. With our swipe feature, employers will be able to screen candidates faster and more transparently. As a freelancer, you will be able to display your work through quality videos.
To create a fast, efficient and simple app that supercharges the hiring process for our users.

Founder

Wallace

Wallace is a grade 10 student with enthusiasm in tech, finance and entrepreneurship. He took the initiative to apply his enthusiasm into a project that could help him hone his skills, and one that could also help people in need of jobs, which lead to him founding “QuickLance”.
